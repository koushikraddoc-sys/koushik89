Thyroid RFA

Radiofrequency Ablation (RFA) is a revolutionary non-surgical treatment that shrinks thyroid nodules using heat energy delivered through a hair-thin needle — under live ultrasound guidance. No incision. No scar. No general anaesthesia. No hospital admission.

Traditionally thyroid nodules required surgery with permanent scarring and risk of voice change. RFA achieves 50–80% nodule volume reduction in a single 30-minute session.

Conditions Treated

  • Benign thyroid nodules causing pressure symptoms
  • Cosmetically bothersome thyroid lumps
  • Recurrent thyroid cysts
  • Low-risk thyroid cancers (selected cases)
  • Hot nodules causing hyperthyroidism

Why Choose RFA Over Surgery

  • No surgical scar on the neck
  • No risk of voice change or hoarseness
  • No lifelong thyroid medication needed
  • Performed under local anaesthesia only
  • Return home within 2 hours

Varicose Vein Treatment

Varicose veins are swollen, twisted veins visible just under the skin — usually in the legs. They cause pain, heaviness, swelling and cosmetic concern. Dr. Koushik treats them with three minimally invasive techniques, all performed as walk-in walk-out day care.

Treatment Options

  • Endovenous Laser (EVLA) — laser fibre inserted into vein, seals it with heat
  • Foam Sclerotherapy — chemical foam injected to collapse and absorb the vein
  • Microwave Ablation — microwave energy seals larger veins precisely

Symptoms This Treats

  • Visible twisted rope-like veins on legs
  • Leg heaviness, aching, swelling
  • Skin discolouration around ankles
  • Night cramps and restless legs
  • Bleeding or ulceration from veins

Slip Disc Injections

A slipped or herniated disc compresses nearby nerves causing intense back pain, leg pain (sciatica), numbness or weakness. Dr. Koushik uses image-guided injections to deliver anti-inflammatory medication precisely to the affected nerve — providing rapid, lasting relief without surgery.

Types of Injections Offered

  • Transforaminal Epidural — medication delivered directly to the nerve root
  • Interlaminar Epidural — covers multiple nerve levels simultaneously
  • Nerve Root Block — diagnostic and therapeutic for specific root pain
  • Disc Hydrodissection — fluid injected around the disc to relieve pressure

Conditions Treated

  • L4-L5 and L5-S1 disc herniation
  • Sciatica and leg radiation pain
  • Cervical disc prolapse with arm pain
  • Spinal canal stenosis
  • Post-surgical back pain

PRP Therapy

Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) therapy harnesses your body's own healing power. A small blood sample is spun in a centrifuge to concentrate the growth factors and platelets, then injected precisely into the damaged joint or tendon under ultrasound guidance — accelerating natural tissue repair.

Conditions Treated with PRP

  • Knee osteoarthritis and cartilage wear
  • Rotator cuff tendinopathy
  • Tennis elbow and golfer's elbow
  • Plantar fasciitis and Achilles tendinopathy
  • Hip labral tears and joint degeneration
  • Patellar tendinopathy (jumper's knee)

PRP Advantages

  • 100% natural — your own blood, no foreign substances
  • Stimulates actual tissue regeneration
  • No systemic side effects
  • Ultrasound-guided for pinpoint accuracy
  • Can delay or avoid joint replacement surgery

Fibroid Embolization

Uterine Fibroid Embolization (UFE) is a minimally invasive alternative to hysterectomy or myomectomy. Tiny particles are injected through a micro-catheter to block the blood supply to fibroids — causing them to shrink — without removing the uterus or any surgery.

Symptoms This Relieves

  • Heavy and prolonged menstrual bleeding
  • Pelvic pressure and bloating
  • Frequent urination from fibroid bulk
  • Anaemia from blood loss
  • Pelvic pain and discomfort

Advantages Over Surgery

  • Uterus preserved — no hysterectomy
  • No surgical incision or scar
  • Treats multiple fibroids in one session
  • Shorter recovery — return to work in 1 week
  • Performed under sedation, not general anaesthesia

Pain Procedures

Chronic pain — whether from cancer, spinal conditions, or nerve damage — can be debilitating. Dr. Koushik offers a full range of image-guided pain management procedures that target the exact pain source without systemic drugs, providing lasting relief and restoring quality of life.

Procedures Offered

  • Coeliac Plexus Block — for upper abdominal cancer pain
  • Facet Joint Denervation (RFA) — burns pain nerves for 12–18 months of relief
  • Trigger Point Injections — for muscle knots causing referred pain
  • Sympathetic Block — for complex regional pain syndrome
  • Intercostal Nerve Block — for rib and chest wall pain
  • Intrathecal Drug Delivery — for advanced cancer pain management

Who Benefits

  • Cancer patients with pain not controlled by medication
  • Chronic back pain unresponsive to physiotherapy
  • Post-surgical nerve pain
  • Diabetic neuropathy with focal pain
